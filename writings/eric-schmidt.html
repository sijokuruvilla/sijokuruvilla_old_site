March 13, 2013

Startup Village is partner to the NASSCOM Start Up Program. The launch of the program is happening at Delhi on the 20th of March, 2013. The program launch is accompanied with a fireside chat with Eric Schmidt, Executive Chairman of Google Inc.

As partner to the program, Startup Village has also been invited and we have decided to extend the opportunity to one of our startups to represent Startup Village at the event. You will have to send in your nominations on or before the 16th March midnight to be eligible for the selection.

Basically you just need to write a blog post to be considered for nomination. All you need to to is to contribute a blog post about any Startup Village experience of yours on the Startup Village blog. For registering yourself as a contributor, write to meera@startupvillage.in with cc to gokul@startupvillage.in

It could be about how Startup Village has helped you in your entrepreneurial journey, it could be about how Startup Village has helped you dream and dream big, it could be about an experience of yours while engaged with the Startup Village community, it could be about one of your fellow entrepreneurs impressive innovation or product, it could be about how Startup Village is making a positive contribution to the society (local, national and global)
